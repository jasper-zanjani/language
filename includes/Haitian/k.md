- **kèk** some (cf. French _quelque_): _Mwen renmen lang e mwen aprann **kèk**_

- **kiyès** who
- **ki kote** where
- **kijan** how
- **kilè** when
- **kisa** what
- **kol** call ; _**kol** video_
- **koman**, **kouman** how : _**kouman** ou ye?_

- **konnen** to know
